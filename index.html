About Me

Get to Know Me

Hi Wassup! My name is Kyanu Omar Eagan, but by default just call me Omar. I was born in Jakarta and was fortunate to be raised in one of the most thrilling and packed city out of all cities, South Tangerang. Growing up, I was sent to a Cambridge Kindergarten by my lovely parents, and I of course have my fair share of muslim & non-muslim accompanies there. Then, elementary freshman year hit.

All of the sudden, everything went upside down 180 degrees. Who was I? Was is my purpose being here? Childhood Omar was once a straight, reserved, and naive kid. Fair share of friends? Let's just say It was a toss-up. But one things for sure, the current Omar is night and day. We'll get to that later... I was such an Introvert, and still am today. But the one thing that stucked to me back then, changed the best of me. Something that I have cherished for so long that I must share will you. My Ambitiousness.

I started to become "Ambitious". Joining competitions, participating in organizations, even running for president. The results? It's a flip of the switch. Now, current senior year Omar will keep on learning and evolving. Destined to become an AI Professional in the future, and what better way to start my journey than to write & create this one of a kind website with love and conscience.

One of my favorite hobbies is to travel abroad. I have been to dozens of unique countries around the globe, like: Australia, Japan, Malaysia and so much more that meets the eye.

I love eating. That's why a journey through the bussing night markets are always right-up my alley. Restaurants? you got that right. Appetizers, Entrees, Desserts, Street-food, you name it.

With an appetite of a giant, I always have to utilize the calories by hitting the reps. I always like to train for Strength & Aesthetics. One of my favorite workouts are Pull-ups, Bicep-curls, and Bench-pressing.

No surprise my Instagram has been blowing-up ever since I publish new art projects. What makes it engaging for me so special is the idea to make this blank sheet of paper/canvas into treasure.

I have many interests across many fields, but one in particular is Public Speaking. Even though I wasn't born with the ability to speak in front of a crowd, I always hold on to my philosophies when nervous.
